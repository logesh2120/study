Our Story

Grown with Love

GreenHaven started in 2010 as a small backyard project. Today, we are one of the city's leading plant nurseries and landscaping firms.

Our mission is simple: To bring a piece of nature into every concrete building. We believe that plants are not just decoration, but a lifestyle that brings peace and health.
