Our Story & Mission

Cultivating green spaces and healthy living since 2010.

Grown with Love

GreenHaven started in 2010 as a small backyard project fueled by a passion for botany. Today, we are one of the region's leading plant nurseries and fully certified landscaping firms, serving hundreds of residential and commercial clients.

Our mission is simple: To bring a piece of nature into every concrete building. We believe that plants are not just decoration, but a lifestyle that brings peace, health, and a tangible connection to the natural world.

  • Premium, locally sourced plants.
  • Certified landscape architects and horticulturists.
  • Commitment to sustainable and organic practices.
